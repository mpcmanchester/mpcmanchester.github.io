ABOUT US

We are an Indian community church with believers from different parts of India. Having come to the UK as working professionals, we gather to worship God.

We are a closely-knit group of born-again spirit-filled believers who are passionate about serving and worshipping the Almighty God, the maker of this Universe. We love people and if you are looking out for a Malayalam cum English Pentecostal church In Manchester, we would be very happy to meet you, worship this great God with you, and share our lives with you.

The Manchester Pentecostal Church was founded in 2004 under the auspices of Pastor Joseph Abraham, with just 5 families. But God has added to our numbers and now we are a church with over 35 families and God is faithful to keep adding more!
